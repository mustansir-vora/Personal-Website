# AI Chatbot Knowledge Base Questionnaire

To build a deeply knowledgeable AI that accurately represents you to potential recruiters, we need high-density context. Please fill out the sections below. You can provide this in Markdown, plain text, or Word format. 

## 1. Professional Identity & Elevator Pitch
*   **Core Identity:** In 2-3 sentences, how do you describe yourself as an engineer?
*   **Unique Selling Proposition (USP):** What makes you stand out from other candidates?
*   **Short-term & Long-term Goals:** What are you looking for in your next role? Where do you want to be in 3 years?

## 2. Logistics & HR
*   **Work Preferences:** Remote, Hybrid, On-site? Willing to relocate (to where)?
*   **Visa/Work Authorization:** Current status (if applicable).
*   **Availability:** Notice period / when can you start?
*   **Compensation:** (Optional) Do you want the bot to handle salary expectation questions, or gently deflect them to a direct conversation with you?

## 3. Behavioral & Soft Skills (The Interview)
Please provide short stories or bullet points for the following (STAR method preferred: Situation, Task, Action, Result):
*   **A Technical Failure:** A time something broke, how you reacted, and what you learned.
*   **Conflict Resolution:** A time you disagreed with a colleague or client and how you resolved it (e.g., during the Rogers IVR or Farmers Insurance projects).
*   **Adaptability:** A time you had to learn a completely new technology under a tight deadline.
*   **Leadership/Mentorship:** Instances where you led a team, mentored someone, or took ownership of a critical feature.

## 4. Deep-Dive: Key Projects
For each major project on your portfolio, please provide:
*   **The "Why":** Why was this specific tech stack chosen over alternatives?
*   **Architecture Breakdown:** A high-level explanation of how the system pieces connect.
*   **Hardest Challenge:** The most difficult bug or architectural hurdle you faced, and exactly how you solved it.
*   **Impact:** Concrete numbers or business impact (e.g., "Reduced hallucinations by 44%").

## 5. Technical Opinions & Philosophies
Recruiters love to hear *how* you think. What are your opinions on:
*   Clean Code & Testing practices.
*   The future of Conversational AI and Agentic workflows.
*   Monoliths vs. Microservices (or similar architectural debates).

## 6. Codebase Context (For the Zipped Files)
When you upload the zipped codebases to the project root, please ensure they include:
*   A brief `README.md` or text file explaining the folder structure.
*   A note on which specific files or functions you are most proud of so the RAG system knows to highlight them.

## 7. Personal Persona
*   **Hobbies:** What do you do outside of work? (Makes the AI feel more human and builds rapport).
*   **Bot Tone:** How should the AI speak on your behalf? (e.g., Professional, enthusiastic, highly technical but accessible).
