About Me

My journey into tech began not with a clear path, but with a formidable challenge. The initial months were a crucible, forging my sense of adaptability and a passion for rapid, continuous learning. It taught me that a person's true measure isn't just their knowledge, but their ability to acquire and master new skills efficiently. This foundation has been my launchpad, enabling me to turn challenges into opportunities and lead complex, high-stakes client integrations.

I'm fluent in core backend technologies like Java, Spring Boot, and Python, comfortable on the frontend with React.js, and adept at building systems with RESTful APIs, SQL, and NoSQL. I confidently navigate multi-cloud environments, leveraging AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud Platform to architect robust solutions. This comprehensive skill set allows me to lead end-to-end development of sophisticated software systems.

While versatile, my true passion lies at the frontier of Artificial Intelligence. I'm captivated by AI's potential to create intuitive human-computer interactions. I specialize in designing and deploying advanced conversational AI systems using platforms like Google Dialogflow (ES/CX), Genesys, and Microsoft Copilot Studio. My work goes beyond simple chatbots/IVR systems; I have engineered complex CX solutions incorporating Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) patterns, and Voice Biometrics for truly context-aware and personalized user experiences. I approach every project with personal ownership, upholding my work to high standards.

Looking ahead, my ambitions extend beyond mere financial success; it is to establish a thriving career that operates with some level of autonomy, giving me the freedom to pursue my passion for global travel and adrenaline-fueled adventures. Ultimately, I seek a future where professional success serves as a means to an end, enabling me to a life rich in experiences, not just professional commitments/obligations. This chase for a balance—where professional achievement enables personal exploration—is what I would define as my ultimate goal.
